Tesura

"Chopperman (orange)" — 2008

2 colour screenprint, acrylic on paper

Limited edition of 2

56 x 76  cm

Chopperman, is he a simple wood chopper or some kind of psycho or both? This is my first ever screen print, hand printed by me on my dodgy home made equipment, as a result it's a very limited edition (as I screwed quite a few up) and available in a variety of colours since I was experimenting a bit. This version is orange. Printed on 300gsm Saunders paper, hand printed, signed and numbered by me.
